About SabayTrip

Travel Smart, Travel Sabay

SabayTrip is a simple and smart travel planning web application designed to help users organize their trips easily. Our goal is to make trip planning more convenient by allowing users to manage destinations, schedules, and important details all in one place.

With features like trip creation, weather checking, and planner management, SabayTrip helps travelers make better decisions and enjoy a smoother travel experience.

This project was developed as a collaborative effort by a team of four students, focusing on user-friendly design, functionality, and real-world usability.
